PhotoWeather App


A Weather application based on openweathermap-api that let the users take a photo,
 add current weather information (e.g. place name, temperature, weather condition, …)
 as a banner overlay on top of the photo and finally, share it.

Functionalities:
1-Capture new photo
2-Save photos
3-Detect current location
4-Request openweathermap-api to get current location weather data.
5-Share the generated image accross applications such as Facebook and Twitter
